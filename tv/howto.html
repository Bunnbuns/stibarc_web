Streaming to STiBaRC TV

Streaming to STiBaRC TV can be easy and fun! Just open up your favorite streaming software (I recommend OBS Studio), go to the streaming settings, and set "rtmp://stibarc.com" as your streaming URL. You do not need a stream key. Next, go to the admin panel and set the stream title. And then you're all set!
